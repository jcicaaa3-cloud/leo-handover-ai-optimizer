# Data Notice

## What data is used?

This project uses synthetic demonstration data generated by `src/leo_handover/simulator.py`.

The simulator creates toy ground users, toy LEO satellite positions, RSRP-like signal values, satellite-load values, remaining visibility estimates, and oracle labels. These values are designed to make the ML pipeline reproducible and discussable, not to reproduce a specific real network.

## What data is not used?

No real satellite operation logs, no customer records, no commercial telecom telemetry, no private network traces, and no production handover decisions are included.

## How to interpret the metrics

The metrics in `docs/evaluation_metrics_example.json` and `docs/RUN_REPORT.md` are smoke-test results on the synthetic simulator. They should not be presented as real-world performance or industrial deployment evidence.
